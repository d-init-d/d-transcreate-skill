# NovaTech Solutions — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Last Updated:** 1 January 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

NovaTech Solutions ("NovaTech," "we," "us," or "our") collects personal data when you create an account, use our cloud-based project management platform (the "Service"), or communicate with our support team. The categories of personal data we collect include:

- **Account Information:** your full name, email address, and encrypted password.
- **Usage Data:** timestamps of login events, features accessed, and duration of sessions.
- **Device Information:** browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
- **Payment Information:** billing address and the last four digits of your payment card. We do not store full card numbers; all payment processing is handled by our certified third-party payment processor.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Contractual Necessity:** processing required to provide the Service you have subscribed to.
- **Legitimate Interest:** analytics and security monitoring to improve service reliability and detect unauthorized access.
- **Consent:** marketing communications, optional cookies, and participation in user research programs. You may withdraw consent at any time through your account settings without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

## 3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

- **Active accounts:** data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus thirty (30) calendar days after account closure.
- **Inactive accounts:** if no login activity is recorded for twelve (12) consecutive months, we will notify you via email and provide sixty (60) days to reactivate before permanent deletion.
- **Legal obligations:** certain records (invoices, consent logs) may be retained for up to seven (7) years to comply with applicable tax and regulatory requirements.

## 4. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, submit a verified request through the Data Privacy section of your account settings or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@novatech-solutions.example.

We will respond to verified requests within thirty (30) days. If additional time is required due to the complexity of the request, we will notify you of the extension and the reasons for the delay.
